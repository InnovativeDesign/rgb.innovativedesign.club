About

Join Innovative Design on Saturday, March 3rd for a day of learning from industry designers, through a series of talks, workshops, and open Q&A. Following the theme of “embracing your roots”, speeches and workshops will explore the unique backgrounds of these professionals and celebrate diversity! The speaker series will be followed by workshops taught by speakers and other designers, and will focus on how to build yourself professionally.

This event will be held at Berkeley’s Jacobs Hall for Design Innovation. Breakfast and lunch will be provided, fun will be had, and minds will be blown.

Speakers

Wayne Spiegel is a Product Designer at Palantir. Originally from Toronto, he was academically trained in graphic design and self-taught in product design.

Wayne values minimalist design as test of his own design foundations. Outside of design, Wayne dabbles in photography, games for hours, and beatboxes in the shower.

Wayne Spiegel

Her essays have been featured in Fast Company, Offscreen Magazine and Thought Catalog. Her first novel, The Firesteel, won First Place for Literary Fiction from Writer’s Digest.

Briana Hokanson is an Experience Designer working on Lightroom and other photography tools at Adobe.

With a multi-disciplinary background in fine arts, design and technology she has worked with clients such as National Geographic, Shinola, & Boston Scientific.
